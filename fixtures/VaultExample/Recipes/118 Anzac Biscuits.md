## Ingredients
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup plain flour
- 1 cup desiccated coconut
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 125 g butter
- 2 tbsp golden syrup
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 2 tbsp boiling water

## Method
1. Mix dry ingredients.
2. Melt butter and syrup, stir in soda water.
3. Combine, shape, and bake until golden.

## Notes
- Rest on the tray for 5 minutes before cooling.
