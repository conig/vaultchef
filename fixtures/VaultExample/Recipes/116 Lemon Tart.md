## Ingredients
- 200 g plain flour
- 120 g unsalted butter, cold, cubed

### Filling
- 4 eggs
- 200 g caster sugar

## Method
1. Make the dough.
2. Blind bake the shell.
3. Bake filling until just set.

## Notes
- Serve with whipped cream.
