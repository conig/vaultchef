## Ingredients
- 500 g spinach
- 250 g paneer
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp garam masala

## Method
1. Blanch spinach and puree.
2. Fry aromatics and spices.
3. Add spinach and paneer, simmer briefly.

## Notes
- Serve with rice or naan.
